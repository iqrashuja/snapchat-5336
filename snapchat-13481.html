    Tricks for Safeguarding Confidentiality on Snapchat App

    Maintain your Snapchat App account exclusive by changing your security settings so as to manage who can access your snaps and stories. Avoid disclosing sensitive information like your location or contact details on open snaps or stories to safeguard your security online.

    Use discretion when agreeing to friend requests on Snapchat App, specifically from unknown individuals. Inform any dubious activity or messages to Snapchat App assistance and stay away from engaging with unsolicited contacts to remain safe while using the app.
